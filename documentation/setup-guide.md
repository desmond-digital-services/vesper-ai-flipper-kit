# Vesper AI Flipper Kit — 60-Second Setup Guide

**Estimated Time: 60 seconds**
**Difficulty: None. Seriously.**

---

## Before You Start

Make sure your phone or tablet has Bluetooth turned on. You'll need it for this.

Have your OpenRouter API key ready. If you don't have one yet, the Vesper app will walk you through getting one — it takes about 2 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Unbox Your Kit

Your kit comes with everything you need inside one box.

**What you'll find inside:**

- **Flipper device** — A small handheld device with a black-and-white screen
- **USB-C charging cable** — Use this to charge your Flipper
- **Quick-start instruction card** — Laminated card with QR codes (keep this!)
- **Any other accessories** listed on your order confirmation

**Photo description:** A open cardboard box on a flat surface. The Flipper device sits in a molded plastic insert at center. A USB-C cable is coiled in the left compartment. A laminated quick-start card sits in the right compartment.

**⚠️ Important:** Remove any protective film from the Flipper screen before you begin. The screen may look hazy if film is still on.

---

## Step 2: Switch the Battery On

Your Flipper ships with the battery switched off to keep it safe during shipping.

**Locate the battery switch** — It's a small slider on the side or back of the device. Look for a switch labeled with "ON" and "OFF."

**Slide it to ON.**

The screen on your Flipper will light up and show the Vesper logo or a "ready" screen within about 5 seconds.

> **If the screen stays dark:** Plug the USB-C cable into a power source and let it charge for 10 minutes. Then try again.

---

## Step 3: Pair Your Flipper Over Bluetooth

Your Flipper connects to your phone or tablet using Bluetooth — no internet required for the connection itself.

### On your Flipper:
1. Use the arrow buttons to scroll to **"Settings"** on the menu
2. Press the **OK/Select button** in the center
3. Scroll to **"Bluetooth"**
4. Press **OK/Select**
5. If Bluetooth shows as "Off," scroll to "On" and press **OK/Select**
6. You should see a device name like **"Flipper-XXXX"** — leave this screen visible

### On your phone or tablet:
1. Open your **Settings** app
2. Go to **Bluetooth** (or **Connections > Bluetooth**)
3. Make sure Bluetooth is turned **On**
4. Wait for your Flipper to appear in the list of available devices
5. Tap on **"Flipper-XXXX"** to pair
6. Confirm the pairing if prompted

> **If you don't see your Flipper:** Make sure the Flipper screen shows "Discoverable" or "Waiting for connection." Give it 10 seconds to appear. Try turning Bluetooth off and back on if needed.

---

## Step 4: Install and Open the Vesper App

1. Scan the QR code on the quick-start card with your phone camera
2. Tap the link that appears to open the download page
3. Download the **Vesper app** (available for Android and iOS)
4. Open the Vesper app
5. Tap **"Add Device"** or **"Connect Flipper"**

The app will guide you through granting Bluetooth permissions if it asks.

> **Android users:** If you see a warning about installing apps from unknown sources, tap **Settings > Allow from this source** — this is normal for sideloaded apps. The app is safe.

> **iOS users:** You may be asked to trust the developer in Settings > General > Device Management.

---

## Step 5: Enter Your OpenRouter API Key

The Vesper app uses the OpenRouter service to power the AI. You need your own API key — it's free to start.

### Getting Your API Key (about 2 minutes):
1. Visit **openrouter.ai** on your phone or computer
2. Tap **"Sign Up"** and create an account (use Google or email)
3. Once logged in, go to **"Keys"** in the top menu
4. Tap **"Create Key"**
5. Give it a name like "Vesper Kit" (optional)
6. Copy the key — it looks like this: `sk-or-v1-a1b2c3d4e5f6...`
7. **Never share this key with anyone.**

### Entering Your Key in Vesper:
1. In the Vesper app, tap **"Enter API Key"**
2. Paste your OpenRouter key
3. Tap **"Save"** or **"Connect"**
4. The app will confirm when the connection is working

> **API Key Error:** If you see "Invalid Key" or "Connection Failed," double-check that you copied the entire key with no extra spaces. Try pasting it into a notes app first to verify, then copy it again.

---

## Step 6: Say Your First Command

Time to try it out.

1. Make sure your Flipper is powered on and connected to your phone via Bluetooth
2. Open the Vesper app if it's not already open
3. Tap the **microphone button** (or type if you prefer)
4. Say or type: **"Read this card"**

The Flipper will respond — watch the screen and listen if your device has speakers.

> **If nothing happens:** Check that your Flipper is still connected in Bluetooth settings. Try disconnecting and reconnecting. Make sure your phone has internet access for this step.

---

## What to Do If Something Goes Wrong

**Flipper screen is dark after switching the battery on:**
- Charge for 10 minutes using the USB-C cable
- Try a different power source
- If still dark after 15 minutes of charging, contact support

**Bluetooth won't pair:**
- Turn Bluetooth off on your phone, wait 10 seconds, turn it back on
- Restart your Flipper by holding the power button for 3 seconds to turn it off, then press it again to turn it on
- Make sure no other device is already connected to your Flipper

**Vesper app can't find your Flipper:**
- Close the Vesper app completely and reopen it
- Check that your Flipper appears in your phone's Bluetooth settings (not just in the app)
- Try forgetting the Flipper from your Bluetooth settings and pairing again

**API key keeps giving an error:**
- Verify you copied the entire key — it's long
- Make sure your OpenRouter account is active (check openrouter.ai)
- Contact support if the key works on the website but not in the app
