## Do I need to know how to code?

**No.** That's the whole point.

The Vesper AI Flipper Kit is built for people who have better things to do than learn to program. If you can use a smartphone, you can use this.

You talk to it like you'd talk to a helpful coworker. You don't need to write any code, run any commands, or know what an API is. The Vesper app handles all of that.

The only slightly technical step is setting up an OpenRouter account to power the AI — but we've designed that to take about 2 minutes, and we walk you through it step by step.

---

## How long does setup take?

**About 60 seconds** if your phone is ready and you have your OpenRouter API key on hand.

If you need to create an OpenRouter account and get an API key first, add about **5 minutes** for that.

After the initial setup, your kit remembers everything. You won't need to pair it or re-enter your API key every time you use it — just turn it on and go.

## How long does the battery last?

The Flipper's battery lasts **about 1 to 2 days** of regular use, depending on how often you're using it.

Tips to extend battery life:
- Turn the Flipper off when you're done using it (hold power button 3 seconds)
- Reduce screen brightness in Settings if it's brighter than you need
- Keep the Flipper connected to Bluetooth — Bluetooth uses less power than searching for new connections

The Moto G Play phone in the kit lasts **all day** on a charge with normal use.

## Does it work without internet?

**Partially.**

The **Bluetooth connection** between the Flipper and your phone works without internet — that's local, direct wireless.

However, the **AI features** require an internet connection. The AI runs on remote servers (powered by OpenRouter), so your phone needs Wi-Fi or mobile data for the AI to respond to your questions and commands.

The included Moto G Play phone connects to Wi-Fi for this.

---

## Does it listen to everything I say?

**Only when you activate it.**

The Vesper app listens when you tap the microphone button or activate a voice command. It doesn't record or transmit audio passively or continuously.

Your voice commands go to OpenRouter's servers to generate a response — that's how the AI works. We don't sell your data or use your voice for anything other than providing the service you signed up for.

For full details, see our **Privacy Policy** at **vespere.ai/privacy**.

If you're working outdoors, keep the kit in a dry bag or protected case when not in use.

## Is the OpenRouter AI free to use?

OpenRouter is **not free**, but it offers a free tier to get started.

Here's how it works:
- OpenRouter charges a small fee for each AI request (typically fractions of a cent per query)
- New accounts get **$1 in free credits** to start — that's enough for hundreds of questions
- After that, you add your own credits as needed. Most casual users spend **$1–5 per month**

You control exactly how much you spend. You can set spending limits in your OpenRouter account.
